<objective>
Replace the fire-and-forget trigger steps in both `deploy-staging` and `deploy-production` jobs of the generated `deploy.yml` with a combined trigger + Coolify status polling step. After triggering a deploy, the generated workflow captures the `deployment_uuid` from the trigger response, polls `/api/v1/deployments/{uuid}` at 10-second intervals (max 36 retries = 6 min), logs `[N/36] status=STATUS` at each poll, and exits non-zero with a clear error message on `status=failed` or timeout — before the health check ever runs.

Purpose: Surfaces failed image pulls within seconds instead of timing out on the health endpoint after 6 minutes.
Output: Modified `scripts/generate-workflow.sh` whose heredoc emits a `deploy.yml` with polling in both deploy jobs.
</objective>
